​Here are some tips on how to get perfectly baked chocolate chip cookies

• bake the cookies in the middle rack to evenly bake

• stick a toothpick in the middle of the cookie to check if it is fully cooked

• don't place the cookies to close together when baking because they will expand and join together

• make sure all ingredients are room temperature to ensure the dough will not be runny
