​ 1. preheat the oven to 375°F

2. In a medium bowl combine flour, baking soda and salt. set that mixure aside for later

3. In a separate large bowl beat together the softened butter, brown sugar and granulated sugar

4. Once fully mixed, beat in the eggs and vanilla extract

5. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix

6. Slowly fold in the chocolate chips

7. Roll out the dough into balls (about a tablespoon each) and place onto the lined baking sheet

8. Bake the cookies for about 10-15 minutes until golden brown.
